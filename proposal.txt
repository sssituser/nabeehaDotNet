Website Functionalities Required
We need a fully custom, high-performance educational website for Rankridge Schools and Rankridge Colleges with an integrated CMS. The website should support easy content updates, SEO management, blog/news publishing, lead tracking, and complete analytics integration. The architecture should be mobile-first.

Core Functionalities Required 

1. Content Management System 

Manage menu, logo, and banners.

Update text and images.

Add and modify categories and subcategories.

Manage pages and sections.

Manage blog posts, news, and announcements.

Manage faculty profiles, courses, and gallery.

Manage FAQs.

Manage contact and enquiry forms.

Upload media such as images, PDF, and documents.

2. SEO Management 

Meta titles and descriptions.

Keywords.

Canonical URLs.

OG tags and social previews.

Structured data (JSON-LD).

Robots.txt and sitemap.xml.

Image Alt tags.

Clean and SEO-friendly URL structure.

3. Lead Management 

Custom enquiry forms.

Lead tracking dashboard.

Export leads to Excel.

Lead status management.

Filters by date, course, and location.

4. WhatsApp and Call Integration 

One-click WhatsApp.

One-click call button.

Optional automated WhatsApp message templates.

5. Blog / News / Updates Module 

Add and edit blogs with images.

Campus updates and articles.

SEO options for each post.

Scheduled publishing.

Social sharing features.

6. Analytics Integration 

Google Analytics.

GA4.

Google Tag Manager.

7. Admin Dashboard 

Manage all content modules.

SEO management area.

Lead management area.

Blog and news manager.

Gallery manager.

Page builder.

System settings.

JWT authentication.

Role-based access.

Activity logs.

8. Security and Compliance 

HTTPS enforced.

SSL support.

OWASP security guidelines.

XSS and SQL Injection protection.

Encrypted sensitive fields.

9. Scalability (Optional Future Features) 

Multi-branch setup.

Student portal.

Online admissions.

Exam system.

Payment gateway.

10. Technology Stack (Recommended) 


Frontend: React.js with TailwindCSS or Material UI.


Optional: Next.js for server-side rendering.


Backend: Node.js + Express.js.


Database: MongoDB (recommended) or MySQL.


Architecture: RESTful API structure.
